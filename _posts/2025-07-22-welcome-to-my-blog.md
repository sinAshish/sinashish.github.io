This is the first post on my new blog! You can write posts in Markdown and include LaTeX math using `$...$` for inline and `$$...$$` for display math.

Example:

Inline math: $E=mc^2$

Display math:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$
